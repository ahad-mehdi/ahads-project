Uncharted 4: A Thief’s End

Released: May 10, 2016 • Platforms: PlayStation 4 • PlayStation 5 • PC

★★★★★ 9.5/10 – Cinematic Masterpiece
Quick Verdict: The most spectacular, emotional, and perfectly paced action-adventure game ever made. Naughty Dog’s glorious send-off to Nathan Drake.

The Review

Uncharted 4 is pure cinema in video game form. From the breathtaking Madagascar jeep chase to the emotional final chapter in Libertalia, every moment feels like a Hollywood blockbuster — but one you get to play.

Nathan Drake’s final adventure delivers the best set pieces in the series, jaw-dropping visuals, and some of the strongest character writing in gaming.

Pros

  • Best set pieces and action sequences in gaming
  • Stunning graphics and animation (still holds up in 2025)
  • Perfect mix of climbing, shooting, and puzzles
  • Emotional, satisfying ending to the series
  • Outstanding voice acting and writing

Cons

  • Combat can feel repetitive near the end
  • Less open than true open-world games

Final Score: 9.5/10

The pinnacle of cinematic adventure gaming.
