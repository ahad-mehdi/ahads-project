Ghost of Tsushima

Released: July 17, 2020 • Platforms: PlayStation 4 • PlayStation 5 • PC

★★★★☆ 9/10 – Visual Masterpiece
Quick Verdict: One of the most beautiful and atmospheric open-world games ever created. A love letter to samurai cinema.

The Review

Ghost of Tsushima is pure art. The wind-guided exploration, golden forests, blood-red maple leaves, and haunting flute soundtrack create an experience unlike anything else.

Jin Sakai’s journey from honorable samurai to the legendary Ghost is powerful and emotional. The duel system, stance switching, and stunning katana combat feel incredible.

Pros

  • Most beautiful open world ever made
  • Satisfying and stylish samurai combat
  • Amazing photo mode and Kurosawa mode
  • Strong story and memorable characters
  • Wind navigation is genius

Cons

  • Side content can feel repetitive
  • Enemy variety is limited

Final Score: 9/10

A breathtaking samurai epic you don’t want to miss.
